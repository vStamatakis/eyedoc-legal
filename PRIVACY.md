# Privacy Policy for eyeDoc

**Last Updated:** 05/03/2026

## Introduction
Evangelos Stamatakis ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. **eyeDoc** was built with a "privacy-first" philosophy. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. All processing related to your breaks, calendar events, and activity status happens locally on your Mac.

## Data Collection and Usage

### 1. Personal Information
eyeDoc does not collect, transmit, or store any personal identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

### 2. Usage Statistics
eyeDoc stores your break statistics (e.g., number of breaks taken, focus streaks) locally on your device using Apple's `UserDefaults`. This data never leaves your computer and is not accessible to us.

### 3. Permissions and Feature Usage

#### Calendar Access
**Why we ask:** To detect when you are in a meeting so eyeDoc can automatically pause the break timer.
**Privacy:** eyeDoc reads your calendar events **locally** on your device. We do not transmit, save, or analyze your calendar details, meeting titles, or attendees.

#### Accessibility (Input Monitoring)
**Why we ask:** To determine if you are "idle" (away from your keyboard/mouse) or "active" (typing/dragging).
**Privacy:** We monitor the *timing* of input events to calculate idle time. We **do not** log your keystrokes, record what you type, or track which websites you visit.

#### Microphone and Camera Access
**Why we ask:** To detect if your microphone or camera is currently in use (e.g., during a Zoom or Teams call) so we can pause the timer.
**Privacy:** eyeDoc checks the **status** (on/off) of your hardware. We **do not** record, listen to, or transmit any audio or video data.

## Payment Data
Payments and subscriptions are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not have access to your credit card information or billing details. We only receive a receipt status to confirm if you have unlocked eyeDoc Pro features.

## Third-Party Services
eyeDoc does not integrate with third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel) or advertising networks.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
